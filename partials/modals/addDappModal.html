Step 1/5 - Preamble

Please read carefully.

You are about to register your decentralized application (dapp) on the Crypti network. After the registration all Crypti users will be able to see your dapp on the Crypti Dapp Store and they will also be able to install and launch it.

One of the characteristics of blockchains is that you can't delete any content from them. Therefore make sure you have used the correct dapp parameters. We also advise you to test your dapp on the official Crypti testnet so you can guarantee that it is working flawlessly.

Things you need to know for the registration process:

- Dapp names are unique, you can't register the same name twice on the Crypti network.

- You can either use a centralized (GitHub) or decentralized (Sia) hosting method.

- Make sure that your dapp icon's format is a square.

- Choose your tags wisely, with them your users will be able to find your dapp in the Dapp Store.

The Crypti Foundation isn't liable for a dapp, solely the uploader is. So you have to make sure that your dapp is legal and doesn't harm its users.
